Professional Projects

This is a collection of my professional projects. I have worked on these projects in a team setting and have learned a lot about collaboration and communication. I hope to continue to grow and learn in this field. I am always looking for new opportunities to expand my skill set and take on new challenges. If you have any questions or would like to discuss potential collaborations, please feel free to reach out to me.

Project 1

Dates Worked On: June 2022

This project consists of logo work for the food truck buisness "The Idaho NutHut". I worked with the owner to create a new look to their logo to put onto shirts for the staff.

Project 2

Dates Worked On: June 2022

This project is a continuation of "The Idaho NutHut" project. The owner asked for fun designs they could use for both shirts and stickers to promote the buisness. I worked for a week with the owners notes in mind to incorporate the colors, phrases and mascots of the buisness into the designs. Owner was incredibly pleased with final product.

Project 3

Dates Worked On: January 2023 - May 2023

This comic panel was apart of a bigger project for school. The comic's theme was quantum machanics featuring Schrodinger's Cat. I worked with a team of four to create all the aspects of this digital interactive comic including art, storyboarding, coding, design and brainstorming.
