Portfolio

Hello, I’m an aspiring animator and art designer from Mountain Home Idaho. I’m enrolled in the GIMM (Gaming Interactive Mobile Media and Technology) program at Boise State University. Here I wish to show off the best of my abilities.

I have a passion for creating and designing characters, and I love to tell stories through my art. I am always looking for new ways to improve my skills and learn more about the industry. I hope to one day work in the gaming or animation industry, where I can bring my ideas to life.
